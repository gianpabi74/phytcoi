# PHYTCOI Cambium Model v1

## Purpose

The Cambium Model defines how a PHYTCOI individual generates growth.

The purpose of Cambium is to transform knowledge into adaptation.

Observation creates evidence.

Evidence creates DNA.

DNA creates understanding.

Understanding creates adaptation opportunities.

Cambium transforms opportunities into growth.

Without Cambium, a PHYTCOI individual may understand its habitat but cannot evolve within it.

---

# Fundamental Principle

Cambium is the growth tissue of the species.

The purpose of Cambium is not execution.

The purpose of Cambium is not governance.

The purpose of Cambium is growth.

Cambium creates structures that allow the individual to become increasingly capable within its habitat.

---

# Core Question

The Cambium Model answers:

How does understanding become growth?

---

# Definition

Cambium is the biological growth engine of a PHYTCOI individual.

Cambium consumes:

* DNA

* germ_* Structures
* Capability Extensions
* Governance Extensions
* Observation Extensions

Cambium is the origin of habitat-specific growth.

# Growth Blueprints

Cambium does not directly create implementation.

Cambium creates blueprints.

A Growth Blueprint defines:

* objective
* source knowledge
* expected benefit
* expected risks
* validation requirements
* confidence

Blueprints describe growth.

They do not execute growth.

---

# Adaptation Generation

Validated Growth Blueprints may generate:

germ_* structures.

Examples:

germ_dns_driver

germ_identity_adapter

germ_storage_capability

germ_recovery_workflow

The generated structures are habitat-specific.

The Genome remains universal.
