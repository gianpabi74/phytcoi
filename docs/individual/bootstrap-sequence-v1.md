# PHYT-001 Bootstrap Sequence v1

## Purpose

This document defines the birth sequence of a PHYTCOI individual.

The Bootstrap Sequence describes how a PHYTCOI Seed becomes a living individual inside a habitat.

Birth is not an event.

Birth is a process.

The objective of the Bootstrap Sequence is to transform species potential into operational reality.

---

# Fundamental Principle

A PHYTCOI individual is not born fully formed.

Knowledge is not inherited.

Understanding is not inherited.

Experience is not inherited.

Adaptations are not inherited.

Only species truth is inherited.

Everything else must be learned.

---

# Core Question

The Bootstrap Sequence answers:

How does a PHYTCOI Seed become a living individual?

---

# Bootstrap Overview

The bootstrap process follows seven major stages.

Stage 0

Seed State

↓

Stage 1

Germination

↓

Stage 2

Embryonic Observation

↓

Stage 3

Knowledge Formation

↓

Stage 4

Trust Formation

↓

Stage 5

Juvenile Individual

↓

Stage 6

Mature Individual

No stage may be skipped.

---

# Stage 0 — Seed State

## Purpose

Preserve species continuity.

## Contents

The Seed contains:

* Constitution
* Mission
* Genome
* Lifecycle
* Species Laws
* Kernel Blueprint
* Germination Mechanisms

The Seed does not contain:

* DNA
* World Model
* Experience
* Adaptations
* Habitat Knowledge

The Seed contains potential.

Not understanding.

---

# Stage 1 — Germination

## Purpose

Initialize a living instance.

## Activities

The Seed is introduced into a habitat.

The Kernel is activated.

Species law becomes enforceable.

Lifecycle management begins.

Communication systems become available.

Observation capabilities become available.

## Result

A Germinating Individual exists.

Knowledge remains empty.

---

# Stage 2 — Embryonic Observation

## Purpose

Observe without intervention.

## Active Organs

* Kernel
* Discovery
* Evidence
* Memory

## Activities

The individual:

* observes habitat objects
* collects evidence
* records observations
* identifies patterns

No execution occurs.

No adaptation occurs.

No governance decisions occur.

## Result

The first evidence corpus emerges.

---

# Stage 3 — Knowledge Formation

## Purpose

Transform observations into habitat truth.

## Active Organs

* Discovery
* Evidence
* Hypothesis
* Memory

## Activities

The individual:

* generates hypotheses
* validates observations
* classifies objects
* builds initial DNA

The first habitat knowledge emerges.

## Result

The DNA begins to exist.

---

# Stage 4 — Trust Formation

## Purpose

Evaluate understanding quality.

## Active Organs

* DNA
* World Model
* Risk
* Governance

## Activities

The individual:

* evaluates confidence
* evaluates uncertainty
* evaluates risks
* evaluates understanding quality

Trust remains minimal until validated.

## Result

Initial trust scores emerge.

---

# Stage 5 — Juvenile Individual

## Purpose

Become capable of limited participation.

## Lifecycle State

NOTOUCH

or

PROGRESSIVE TRUST ASSESSMENT

depending on validation quality.

## Activities

The individual may:

* provide recommendations
* explain observations
* explain risks
* suggest adaptations

Execution remains constrained.

## Result

A juvenile PHYTCOI individual exists.

---

# Stage 6 — Mature Individual

## Purpose

Operate as a habitat steward.

## Lifecycle State

FIX_AND_APPLY

## Requirements

The individual possesses:

* validated DNA
* stable World Model
* operational Experience
* governance maturity
* adaptation maturity

## Activities

The individual may:

* govern
* adapt
* recover
* maintain
* optimize

within governance and security boundaries.

## Result

A mature PHYTCOI individual exists.

---

# Failure Conditions

Bootstrap failure conditions include:

* habitat incompatibility
* insufficient observation
* invalid evidence
* corrupted DNA
* incomplete World Model
* premature autonomy

Failure must halt progression.

---

# Recovery Procedures

Recovery mechanisms include:

* restart germination
* rebuild DNA
* rebuild World Model
* replay evidence
* rollback lifecycle state

Recovery preserves continuity.

---

# Relationship with NEST

The habitat exists before germination.

The habitat is not created by the individual.

The individual must first understand the habitat.

Only then may it interact with the habitat.

---

# Relationship with Species

The Bootstrap Sequence is species law.

All future individuals must follow this process.

Future implementations may differ.

The bootstrap principles may not.

---

# Relationship with Future Generations

PHYT-001 is the first reference implementation.

Future individuals may improve implementation details.

The maturation sequence remains unchanged.

---

# Long-Term Vision

The Bootstrap Sequence exists to ensure that every PHYTCOI individual begins life safely.

The species must never produce individuals that act before understanding.

Understanding must always precede authority.

Wisdom must always precede autonomy.

---

# Final Rule

A PHYTCOI individual is not born when the Seed is installed.

A PHYTCOI individual is born when observation becomes understanding.

Installation creates potential.

Learning creates life.

Understanding creates individuality.

Only then does a true PHYTCOI individual exist.
