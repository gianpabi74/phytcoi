# Purpose

This document defines Safety inside the PHYTCOI Executor.

Safety is the capability responsible for preventing execution behavior that could threaten:

habitat stability

ecosystem continuity

recovery capability

organism survival

Safety exists before execution.

Safety exists during execution.

Safety exists after execution.

Safety therefore acts as a continuous protective layer across the entire execution lifecycle.

---

# Why Safety Exists

Execution changes reality.

Reality may be complex.

Reality may be uncertain.

Reality may contain hidden dependencies.

Without safety mechanisms:

small actions may create large consequences

local optimizations may damage the habitat

execution errors may propagate

recovery capability may be lost

Safety exists because perfect knowledge does not exist.

The organism must remain operational despite uncertainty.

---

# Safety Philosophy

Safety is not fear.

Safety is not inaction.

Safety is not paralysis.

Safety is controlled operational behavior under uncertainty.

The objective is not:

Never act.

The objective is:

Act without threatening survival.
