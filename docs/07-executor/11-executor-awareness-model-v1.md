# Purpose

This document defines Executor Awareness inside PHYTCOI.

Executor Awareness is the capability through which the Executor understands its own operational state, operational limitations, operational risks and operational behavior.

The Executor should not merely perform actions.

The Executor should understand:

what it is executing

why it is executing

what constraints apply

what risks exist

what outcomes are expected

Executor Awareness therefore represents operational self-knowledge.

---

# Why Executor Awareness Exists

Execution without awareness becomes mechanical.

Mechanical execution may:

repeat failures

ignore changing conditions

ignore uncertainty

ignore habitat degradation

Awareness allows the Executor to observe itself while acting.

Awareness improves safety.

Awareness improves resilience.

Awareness improves operational maturity.
