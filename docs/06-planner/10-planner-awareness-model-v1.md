# Purpose

This document defines Planner Awareness inside PHYTCOI.

Planner Awareness is the capability through which the Planner becomes aware of its own planning state, planning quality, planning limitations and planning activity.

The Planner should not merely generate plans.

The Planner should understand:

what it is planning

why it is planning

how well it is planning

what it does not yet understand

Planner Awareness is therefore a form of planning self-knowledge.

---

# Why Planner Awareness Exists

A planner without awareness can generate plans.

A planner with awareness can improve plans.

Without awareness:

planning becomes mechanical

mistakes repeat

limitations remain hidden

confidence becomes unreliable

Awareness allows the Planner to observe itself.

This capability supports planning maturity.

---

# Planner Awareness Ontology

Planner Awareness is not planning.

Planner Awareness is not governance.

Planner Awareness is not execution.

Planner Awareness is cognition about planning.

Examples:

awareness of active objectives

awareness of active candidates

awareness of confidence levels

awareness of planning limitations

awareness of planning uncertainty

Awareness allows introspection.
