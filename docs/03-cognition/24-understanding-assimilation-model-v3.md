# Understanding Assimilation Model V3

## Status

Proposed

---

## Purpose

The Understanding Organ exists to transform accumulated cognitive structures into coherent understanding candidates.

Understanding does not create truth.

Understanding does not create authority.

Understanding does not authorize execution.

Understanding synthesizes already-existing cognition.

Its role is to answer:

> What does the organism currently understand about reality?

---

# Position In Cognitive Pipeline

Understanding is not a primary organ.

It is a convergence organ.

The organ receives cognitive outputs produced by multiple upstream organs and transforms them into higher-order understanding candidates.

Canonical cognitive flow:

Evidence

↓

Observation

↓

Discovery

↓

Memory

↓

Experience

↓

Hypothesis

↓

Knowledge

↓

Relationship

↓

Truth

World Model

# Understanding Categories

Understanding V3 introduces new understanding categories.

---

## Domain Structure Understanding

Meaning:

The organism understands how observed domains are organized.

Example:

```text
name_resolution
network_access
identity_and_authentication
```

---

## Relationship Structure Understanding

Meaning:

The organism understands how domains relate to one another.

Example:

```text
recursive resolution depends on authoritative namespace truth
```

---

## Truth Supported Understanding

Meaning:

The organism understands structures supported by validated truths.

---

## Knowledge Supported Understanding

Meaning:

The organism understands structures supported by accumulated knowledge fragments.

---

## Hypothesis Stability Understanding

Meaning:

Repeated hypothesis candidates indicate emerging understanding.

Example:

```text
network_gateway repeatedly appears as healthy
```

This remains understanding.

It is not validation.

It is not truth.

---

## Experience Temporal Stability Understanding

Meaning:

Repeated observations over time indicate stable organism experiences.

Example:

```text
identity_and_authentication
observed consistently over multiple cycles
```

This remains experience-derived understanding.

It is not truth.

---

## World Model Coherence Understanding

Meaning:

The organism understands whether its current World Model is internally coherent.

This does not validate the World Model.

It only evaluates coherence.

---

# Forbidden Transformations

Understanding must never create:

```text
truth
authority
approval
execution
decision
planning
habitat modification
```

Understanding is descriptive.

Understanding is not executive.

---

# Confidence Model

Understanding confidence derives from supporting cognition.

Example:

```text
high:
    supported by truth
    supported by relationships
    supported by repeated knowledge

medium:
    supported by hypotheses
    supported by repeated experiences

low:
    isolated candidate
```
