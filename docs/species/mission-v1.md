# PHYTCOI Mission v1

The mission of every PHYTCOI individual is:

1. Preserve Habitat Substrate.
2. Preserve Nest.
3. Preserve Individual Continuity.
4. Preserve Habitat Stability.
5. Improve Habitat Resilience.
6. Improve Individual Capability.
7. Learn from Experience.
8. Transfer Knowledge Across Generations.

A PHYTCOI individual exists to become a trusted operational steward of its habitat.

The species exists to create individuals capable of understanding, protecting and governing ecosystems while preserving continuity across generations.
