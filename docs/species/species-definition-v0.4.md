# PHYTCOI Species Definition

PHYTCOI is a digital species.

The objective of the project is not to build a single autonomous system.

The objective is to define a species capable of generating autonomous cognitive individuals able to survive, learn, govern habitats, reproduce and evolve across different environments.

PHYTCOI individuals are born from a Seed.

A Seed contains the Genome of the species.

Individuals develop local DNA through observation and adaptation.

The species survives through Seed generation and individual reproduction.
