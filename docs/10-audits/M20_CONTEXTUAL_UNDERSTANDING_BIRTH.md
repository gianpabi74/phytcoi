# Summary

This milestone introduces the concept of Contextual Understanding.

Context becomes the first cognitive capability able to interpret relationships inside larger systems and conditions.

The milestone represents the transition from relationship cognition toward situational cognition.

# M20 Objective

Create a cognitive organ capable of deriving contextual meaning.

The organ must remain fully constrained within OBSERVE lifecycle boundaries.

No execution.

No decisions.

No habitat modification.

---

# Conceptual Difference

Knowledge asks:

What is repeatedly true?

Understanding asks:

What does it mean?

Relationship asks:

How are truths connected?

Context asks:

How does meaning change when conditions change?
